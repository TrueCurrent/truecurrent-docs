## What makes TrueCurrent different

**Zero trading fees for users.** You pay nothing to open or close positions. No taker fees, no gas fees, no hidden charges.

**Best-price execution.** When you trade, TrueCurrent automatically collects prices from professional liquidity providers and executes at the best one. You set a price floor, and we handle the rest.

**Truly non-custodial.** Unlike most exchanges, your funds never move into a wallet controlled by another entity. Your assets stay in your own wallet at all times – TrueCurrent never takes custody.

**Sub-second settlement.** Trades finalize onchain in under one second.
