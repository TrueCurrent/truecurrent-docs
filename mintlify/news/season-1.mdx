## **How Season 1 works**

Season 1: The Surge begins on **May 11, 2026**.

### **1. Earn TC Points exclusively through TrueCurrent**

TC Points are earned exclusively through the TrueCurrent web app and mobile which is coming soon. They accumulate based on your trading activity. Unlike traditional points systems that reward raw volume, TC Points reflect how much your trading contributes to market quality, risk balancing, and protocol health.

### **2. 10,000 TC Points distributed weekly**

10,000 TC Points are distributed every week. Each epoch runs from Monday 00:00 UTC to Sunday 23:59:59 UTC. At the end of each epoch, the fixed pool is divided among all active traders based on their relative contribution. TC Points are finalized and displayed 24 hours after each epoch ends, so you will not see your points update in real time.

## **How TC Points are earned**

TC Points accrue through using the TrueCurrent web app, with trading activity as the main driver. Each trade is scored based on a composite of factors, including trade size and protocol contribution. Your weekly TC Points reflect your relative score compared to all other traders in that epoch.

\
**Referral program**

When someone uses your referral code, they gain access to TrueCurrent and are permanently linked to you as their referrer.

You can create and manage your codes from the referral dashboard on the web app.

**How referral TC Points work:**

Your referral network directly boosts your weekly TC Points. For every user referred, 20% of their TC Points score is added to yours before the weekly pool is distributed. This means active referrals meaningfully increase your share – without reducing your referrals' own earnings. Every user you refer earns for you. 

Referral relationships are permanent and immutable. Once someone joins using your code, you benefit from their activity going forward. Only their trading activity after registration counts toward your referral bonus. You cannot enter another user’s referral code if you’ve already entered one.
