## Email

For direct support inquiries, reach out to **[support@tc.xyz](mailto:support@tc.xyz)**. The team typically responds within one business day.

## Before you reach out

To help us resolve your issue faster, please include:

- Your **wallet address** (Injective or EVM)
- A clear **description of the issue** or question
- Any relevant **transaction hashes** or error messages
- **Screenshots** if the issue is visual or UI-related

You can also check the [FAQ](/faq) for answers to common questions about trading, deposits, wallet authorization, and more.
